Protube VR Compatability List

Guide

PN3
Pico Neo 3. Direct game links for this platform are not available through a web browser.
NW
Currently not working as intended and requires maintenance from the developer. Visible only when signed in.
FTN
ForceTube No. Compatible with ForceTube, but its use is not recommended for the best gameplay experience.
PVN
ProVolver No. Compatible with ProVolver, but playing with ProVolver is not recommended due to lack of handgun haptic feedback.
PVX2
ProVolver x2. Supports dual-wielding with two ProVolver accessories.
PTN
ProTommy No. Technically compatible with a combination of ForceTube and ProVolver on a MagTube, but not recommended.
PTNC
ProTommy Not Compatible. Does not support the combination of ForceTube and ProVolver on a MagTube.
Note:

If you do not see a game you play on this list or the mods list above in important links, there is a backwards compatibility tool specifically for SteamVR/OpenVR. Otherwise, hit up the devs of the game in question and request they add support

Why I made this

I really wanted an easy way to display the compatability list, and having a quick table I could search or check on my phone quickly to see what game works with the ProtubeVR provoler. It was really hard to look though the thread on reddit, and is confusing to someone new and just wanting a quick answer. I hope you find it as useful as its been for me, made this for fun and pull requests are welcome to update, this data is all based off a CSV thats pretty easy to understand. Just open in your fav spreadsheet editor and add/remove what you want. I don't use reddit at the moment so anyone is welcomed to post about this site.
